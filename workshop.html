Seminars & Webinars

Webinar: SPADE x AIESEC Digital Marketing Skill-Building Session

On 13th March 2025, SPADE, in collaboration with AIESEC in Delhi University, successfully conducted a LIVE online skill-building session on Digital Marketing. The session featured Mr. Nafis Khan, an industry expert in Cold Outreach and AI-driven Marketing, and was aimed at equipping participants with practical strategies relevant to the current digital landscape.

A Seminar: on Career Insights for success by SPADE

On 17th February 2025, SPADE, Shivaji, organized an engaging seminar on cracking competitive exams and mastering career communication. The session featured renowned career strategist Meenakshi Aggarwal, who shared valuable insights on exam strategies, time management, and professional communication.
