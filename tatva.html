What is Tatva?

"Tatva" is our student-led E-magazine offering a refreshing voice in economics. It brings together diverse perspectives, encouraging young minds to question, explore, and reshape their understanding of the world through original, student-driven academic work.

Explore our inaugural issue focusing on a fascinating intersection of disciplines.

Edition 1: Neuroeconomics

Our 2025 edition delves into the captivating field of Neuroeconomics. This issue features 20 student-authored articles that explore the neural underpinnings of decision-making, cognitive biases in financial markets, and the psychology behind our economic choices. Discover how brain activity shapes markets and individual behavior.
