About S.P.A.D.E

SPADE, Founded in 2016, set up in 2017, is the Society for Practical Application and Development of Economics, the ed-research society of Shivaji College. Built on a clear blueprint and a shared vision, it is a vibrant community where students come together to solve real-world problems. Using a research-driven approach, SPADE empowers its members to apply critical thinking and creativity to real-world issues. Through collaborative projects like documentaries, publications, and events, the society inspires its members to turn data into dialogue. "At SPADE, we're not just a society, we're a community that builds, leads, fails but builds anyway"

Our Project

Documentary

Our in house documentary features leading voices of change makers, experts, and entrepreneurs sharing individual insights on the issues of the world. Through real life storytelling and guest interviewees, SPADE records various opinions as well as their personal experiences. They produce films that educate, inspire, and ignite important conversations; driving change and expanding knowledge.

Tatva, which means "essence," is an in-house publication by SPADE. It is a student-led magazine that dives deep into issues that matter. It is a magazine wherein students actively contribute by publishing their own articles. Arthamanthan

Arthamanthan is SPADE's annual ISBN-published research booklet featuring student led research on contemporary issues relevant to society. Renowned for analytic depth and originality, it provides a credible platform for young researchers to have ideas presented, perspectives honed, and valuable contributions to scholarly thought and social insight annually.

Ace of Spade

Ace of Spade is our most awaited annual event, wherein we bring together curiosity, creativity, and collaboration. The event further features speaker sessions, our publication launch, documentary screenings, case competitions, and other exciting inter-college contests. All of this comes together in a vibrant celebration of creativity, innovation, and teamwork, making Ace of Spade an unforgettable experience for everyone.

Workshops

SPADE organizes practical and skill building workshops that empower students with hands on knowledge and industry insights. The workshops are based on topics from data analysis and research writing to career strategies and digital marketing. These sessions provide students with skills, expert guidance, and opportunities to enhance both academic and professional growth.

Seminars

Our seminars provide interactive workshops with experts, exploring evolving job trends like freelancing and short-term projects. Through interviews and knowledge sharing, these sessions highlight future directions in the workforce while giving students valuable learning experiences from some of the best in the field.
